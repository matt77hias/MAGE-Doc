§ m_force_srgb

bool mage::SpriteFontDescriptor::m_force_srgb
private

A flag indicating whether working around gamma issues is needed for this sprite font descriptor.

This is helpful for color fonts that are in the sRGB or similar color space but are not encoded explicitly as an SRGB format.
