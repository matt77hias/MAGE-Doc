§ m_dissolve

f32 mage::Material::m_dissolve
private

The amount this material dissolves into the background.

A factor of 1.0 is fully opaque. A factor of 0.0 is fully dissolved (completely transparent).

Unlike a real transparent material, the dissolve does not depend upon material thickness nor does it have any spectral character.
